#project

Project Zenith is focused on launching a constellation of atmospheric weather prediction satellites. it is the lead for this initiative. With a budget of $761k, the project employs it as its main operational tool. The objective is to significantly improve the accuracy and scope of global weather forecasting capabilities.

## Key facts
- Goal: Launch a constellation of atmospheric weather prediction satellites.
- Lead: [[Ethan Cross]]
- Run by [[Veridian Robotics]]
- Built with [[NeuroLink OS]]
- Budget: $761k
