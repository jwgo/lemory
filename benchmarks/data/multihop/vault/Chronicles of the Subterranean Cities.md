#book

I'm revisiting "Chronicles of the Subterranean Cities," a significant book from 1997 by it. This work explores the fascinating topic of Future Urban Planning, specifically focusing on the concept and implications of cities built beneath the earth's surface. Singh's vision offers a unique perspective on sustainable living and resource management in densely populated future environments. It's a classic in speculative urbanism.

## Key facts
- Topic: Future Urban Planning
- Published: 1997
- Written by [[Maya Singh]]
